Domestic Animal Abuse and Adoption

PAWS

It is our mission to end abuse/ homelessness of domestic animals one city at a time!

My name is Akshita and I am the founder of the Pet and Animal Welfare Society, or PAWS.


Some Suprising Statistics/ Facts

  1. Dogs are the most commonly abused animals (65% of known animal cruelty cases).

  2. Specific breeds of dogs, such as pitbulls, are abused more compared to other breeds.

  3. Of the 6.5 million (approximatley) dogs and cats that are brought to anima shelter in the U.S. yearly, 1.5 million are euthanized.
